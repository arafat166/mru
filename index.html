About the Faculty

The Faculty of Science and Technology at Muteesa 1 Royal University (MRU) in Uganda offers various programs in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) fields. Some potential areas of focus might include: Undergraduate and graduate programs in sciences like biology, chemistry, physics, technology and engineering programs, research initiatives, and community outreach and partnerships.

Muteesa 1 Royal University has multiple campuses, including one in Masaka and another in Kampala.
